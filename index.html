Qixu Chen

CS PhD Candidate

The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology

I am a CS PhD candidate at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), specializing in data management and human-in-the-loop systems. My research focuses on improving data quality through novel constraint-based approaches and developing robust methods for handling unreliable user feedback in database systems.

I have published papers in top-tier database conferences including SIGMOD, VLDB, and ICDE. I am also a recipient of the prestigious Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme.
